Welcome to the New Jersey COVID-19 Website

Here, you can find essential information regarding the impact of COVID-19 throught New Jersey. On the map below, request help or offer help to those near you.

First Time? Returning User Learn More about COVID-19

GET STARTED

Need something?

Fill out the form under the map to add a red marker.

Want to become a volunteer?

Fill out the "Offer Help" form to add a blue marker displaying your details to the map.

Want to help?

Click on a marker to contact the requester. From there, you can obtain their personal information to complete their request.

What volunteers are near you?

When you click on your red marker, you will see all the blue markers surrounding you.

Want to delete your marker?

Fill out the form which you used to add a marker. Enter in the same information as you did to generate the markers. Before submitting the form, select "Delete Marker" on the form.
